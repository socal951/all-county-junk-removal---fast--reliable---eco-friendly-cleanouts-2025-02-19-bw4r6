Frequently Asked Questions

What items do you accept?

We accept most household and commercial items, including furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, and general junk. The only items we can't take are hazardous materials like chemicals, paint, and asbestos.

How does your pricing work?

Our pricing is based on the volume your items occupy in our truck, with rates starting at $99. We provide free, no-obligation estimates on-site and guarantee no hidden fees or surprises.

How quickly can you respond?

We offer same-day service in most areas, with typical response times within 2-4 hours for urgent requests. Regular appointments can be scheduled at your convenience, including weekends.

What areas do you serve?

We service all major metropolitan areas and surrounding suburbs within a 50-mile radius. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

How do you handle recycling and disposal?

We partner with local recycling centers and charities to ensure maximum resource recovery. Our team sorts items for recycling, donation, and proper disposal, with up to 70% of materials diverted from landfills.
